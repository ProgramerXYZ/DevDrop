requests, commits, assets, workflows, configuration files, and
derivative works.

The Contributor irrevocably assigns all rights, title, and interest in
the Contribution to the Repository Owner.

The Contributor assigns and licenses all patent rights embodied in the
Contribution.

The Contributor waives attribution and integrity rights to the maximum
extent permitted by law.

The Contributor represents that they possess authority and ownership to
submit the Contribution.

## Article 6 --- Indemnification

The Contributor indemnifies the Repository Owner against claims arising
from the Contribution.

## Article 7 --- Limitation of Liability

The Repository Owner shall not be liable for indirect or consequential
damages.

## Article 8 --- Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by applicable law selected by the
Repository Owner.

## Article 9 --- Dispute Resolution

Disputes shall be resolved in a forum selected by the Repository Owner.
